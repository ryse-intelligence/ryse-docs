# The 60-Second Logistics Profitability Audit

_Are manual processes capping your growth? Score your daily operations._

**Instructions:** Check the box that best describes your current daily operations.

### Section 1: The Morning Rush (Data & Dispatch)

Fragile: We spend over 1 hour daily manually standardizing client data (CSVs, Portals) before routes can even be planned.

Stable: Data intake is partially automated, but still requires manual review and formatting.

Optimised: Client data is ingested, standardized, and ready for dispatch automatically in seconds.

### Section 2: Route Execution & Mid-Day Exceptions

Fragile: When a truck breaks down or a rush order drops, it takes 30+ minutes of manual phone calls and white-boarding to re-route.

Stable: We have software, but dispatchers still have to manually drag-and-drop stops to fix mid-day issues.

Optimised: The system autonomously proposes optimized re-routes instantly when exceptions occur.

### Section 3: The Last-Mile Leak (First-Time Delivery)

Fragile: Our First-Time Delivery (FTD) failure rate is over 10%. We only know about a failed delivery _after_ the driver leaves the doorstep.

Stable: We send generic tracking links, but still suffer from high recipient no-shows.

Optimised: We use predictive text and automated workflows to confirm recipient availability _before_ the truck arrives.

### Section 4: End-of-Day Overhead (Reconciliation)

Fragile: It takes 1–2 hours daily to manually cross-reference Proof of Delivery (PODs) with manifests to generate invoices.

Stable: We have a system, but billing disputes and high Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) are still common.

Optimised: PODs automatically reconcile against manifests, generating flawless, dispute-free invoices instantly.

If you checked **"Fragile"** in two or more categories, your business is likely losing profits and customers to invisible operational friction (redundant fuel, labor, and administrative overhead).
