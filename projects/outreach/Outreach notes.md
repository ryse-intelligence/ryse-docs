# Subject lines

⏺ Pain/curiosity angle

- Still doing this manually?
- Where's your team losing the most time?
- The productivity gap — are you feeling it?
- What would your team do with 10 extra hours a week?

Credibility + intrigue

- From Atlassian to your industry
- What I'm seeing from inside the AI world
- The adoption curve — where does your business sit?

Direct / low friction

- 20 minutes?
- Quick question about your operations
- Worth a conversation?

Slightly provocative

- Your competitors are moving. Are you?
- AI is cheaper than you think
- The window is shorter than most people realise
